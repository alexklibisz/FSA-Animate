FSA-Animate

FSA-Animate is a tool for visualizing the conversion of a nondeterministic finite automataton (NFA) to a deterministic finite automaton.

FSA-Animate was developed by Alex Klibisz and Connor Minton as our Honors By Contract Project for COSC 312, Spring 2015, University of Tennessee, Knoxville.
